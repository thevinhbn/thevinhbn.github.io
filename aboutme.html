About me

I completed my PhD on KKM Theory under the supervision of Prof. Do Hong Tan at Institute of Mathematics, VAST in 2011. Previously, I gained my both BS (2002) and MS (2004) in Mathematics from VNU University of Science. I am currently a lecturer of Department of Mathematics at the University of Transport and Communications (UTC). My current research interests include:

  • Convex Analysis and Optimization Algorithms

  • Compressive Sensing (CS), Signal and Image Restoration

  • Stochastic Optimization Methods for Machine Learning

  • Dynamical System Method for Optimization

  • Equilibrium and Variational Inequality, Fixed Point Problems: Theory and Algorithms
